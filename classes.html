Four Ways
to Train

Every class is led by a certified instructor and built around real intensity — pick the discipline that matches your goal, or rotate through them all.

01

Cardio · Low Impact

Spin

High-intensity, low-impact indoor cycling that targets endurance, leg strength, and recovery. Heart-pounding playlists, real coaching, and serious calorie burn — every ride.

02

Conditioning · Full Body

HIIT & Bootcamp

Explosive interval training that torches calories long after the session ends. Full-body circuits run on our rooftop track — built to spike your fitness fast.

03

Mobility · Mindful

Power Yoga & Pilates

Mindful movement that builds core strength, flexibility, and balance. Controlled flows with a focus on injury prevention — recovery and performance in one practice.

04

Strength · Performance

Strength & Conditioning

Coached resistance training and athletic conditioning to build muscle, power, and durability. Structured progression for every level — from first-timer to competitor.
